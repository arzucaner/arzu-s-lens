Who I am?

Arzu began her career in the telecommunications sector, specializing in CRM systems and marketing. After earning her master's degree in marketing, she successfully led customer loyalty and retention projects as a Marketing and Customer Relationship Manager. She developed and implemented strategic campaigns aimed at enhancing customer satisfaction and loyalty. Then she made significant contributions as a digital project manager, supporting the growth and development of startups, and she has a strong background in SEO. Transitioning into software, Arzu focused on databases. Currently, her focus is on mobile and web application development, where she innovates solutions to improve user experience and performance. She is actively involved in artificial intelligence projects, particularly in natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning, as a "Prompt Engineer." Arzu shares her career journey and technical insights through blog posts and serves as an evangelist in the Women Coding Community, actively promoting the advancement of women in technology.

My Projects

  • When Women Understand Description: The "When Women Understand" project is a website design initiative aimed at helping women recognize not only physical violence but also psychological abuse, to empower them to protect themselves from such forms of violence. This project underscores the often silent and hidden nature of psychological abuse, making it easy for women to recognize. Designed for the Women Who Code Hackathon Social Good 2023, this project won the "Outstanding Social Impact Award" for its empowerment of women. Role: Fullstack Developer (Team Lead) Technologies Used: Node.js, MongoDB, JavaScript, React, Bootstrap, Passage by 1Password Link: When Women Understand Project on GitHub
  • TravelBrew Description: TravelBrew is a mobile application designed to assist travellers in discovering the best coffee and cosy spots in the places they visit. It empowers users to explore top-notch venues that cater to their essential needs, such as coffee, sandwiches, seating areas, and restrooms. This app, designed for the WWC App Deploy Hackathon 2023, houses the source code and assets for locating nearby gas stations, cafés, and restaurants. Role: Fullstack Developer Technologies Used: React Native, Expo, Firebase, Google Maps API Link: TravelBrew Project on GitHub
